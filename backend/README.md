## EasyLedger is a cloud-based, automated invoice and payment tracking solution

## Data Requirements

- Invoices
- Payments
- Clients/Customer
- Notifications
- Permissions

## Data Fields

### Invoices

- Invoice Number
- Client ID
- Due Date
- Amount Due
- Status (Draft, Open, Paid, Void)
- Notes

### Clients/Customer

- Client Name
- Point of Contact
- Email
- Phone
- Address

### Notifications

- Related Invoice Number
- Sent Date
- Email Address

### Payments

- Related Invoice Number
- Amount Paid
- Payment Date
- Notes
